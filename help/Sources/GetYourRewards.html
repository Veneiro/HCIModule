Getting the rewards

Adding the items you want to the list

To spend your point you have to select one item of the combobox and click on the add button. This item will appear on the list at the left.

Removing the items from the list if you want to add a diferent item

If you want to remove from the list one of the items you have to select it in the list and then click on the remove button next to the add button. The points will go back to you to spend them in other items
