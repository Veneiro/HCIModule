How To Play

How the game work

On the game you have 25 boxes with the prizes randomly distributed, you will unveil 3 of them and after pressing Continue you will know if you got some points or not
 
Availible Prizes:
  • 1000 points box
  • 250 points box
  • 50 points box
  • Boxes that double your final points
  • Boxes that give you one more try to unveil a box

The obtained points will be revealed at the end of the game
