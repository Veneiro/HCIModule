Travel - Start Date

How to select the start date for my travel

To select the date for a travel you just have to do the following:
  1. You can select the travel at the top
  2. Select a Date on the calendar below the travel name
  3. And then you have two options
    1. Click on Assign Date and click continue if you only have one travel or just select another travel if you have more than one to assing the date
    2. Write some observations before leaving. Allergies, Health issues...
  4. After that just press continue and you will get your prizes at the stand next to the terminal

When I will get my ticket for the travel

The tickets take longer to came to you. At the stand you will be asked for some data to keep in contact with you. You will be notice as soon as we have the travel booked for you. Usually this proccess only take 1 or 2 days
